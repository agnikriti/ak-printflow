The Web-to-Print Gap

Standard design tools build layouts in digital pixels, not physical units.

This disconnect leads to costly manufacturing defects at the print shop:

  • Clipping Risks: Important text or logo borders get sliced off by mechanical paper guillotines because layout offsets weren't validated.
  • Color Drift: Neon screen colors (RGB) print as dark, muddy shades due to offset printer ink boundaries.
  • Fuzzy Images: Low-resolution logo and barcode files look pixelated and blurry when scaled on paper.
